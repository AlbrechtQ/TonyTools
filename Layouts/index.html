Defimons Battle Calculator.

This tool allows you to calculate your position of advantage/disadvantage of your Mon relative to any enemy Mon. It utilizes strength and weakness charts for both primary and secondary type. It converts the position to a scale from -4 (massive disadvantage) to 4 (massive advantage) with 0 being equal.

It also calculates the best possible match against the enemy Mon, which has advantage in both types.
